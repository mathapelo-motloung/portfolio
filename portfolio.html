Vehicle Breakdown Assistance
Vehicle Breakdown Assistance

Vehicle Breakdown Assistance is a web application that was built to assist users to search for and find mechanics within their location incase of a vehicle breakdown, or any other situation that would require the assistance of a mechanic. Mechanics are able to respond to the users request and indicate on the app if they will attend to the client. Both the Mechanic and the Client are able to provide feedback on the app.


Technologies
  • Springboot
  • MySQL RDBMS
  • Jsp and Jstl
  • Hibernate ORM
  • Jenkins
